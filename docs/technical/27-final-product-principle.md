# 27. Final Product Principle

Ventier does not need the most complex website. It needs a website that:

- Explains the products quickly.
- Presents car carpet visuals effectively.
- Makes the brand look credible.
- Helps potential customers take action.
- Is easy for administrators to update.
- Remains fast, secure, and stable in production.

Any feature that does not support these outcomes must be challenged before development begins.
