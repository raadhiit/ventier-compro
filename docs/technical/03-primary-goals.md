# 03. Primary Goals

## Business Goals

- Increase Ventier brand awareness and credibility.
- Present the car carpet catalog clearly and attractively.
- Help potential customers find relevant products.
- Direct visitors toward a clear conversion action, especially contacting Ventier.
- Allow administrators to perform routine content updates without developer assistance.
- Support promotions, campaigns, and article publishing.

## Technical Goals

- Deliver a stable and maintainable website.
- Use pragmatic Laravel architecture without unnecessary abstractions.
- Use Livewire for useful interactions without making the entire website dependent on complex JavaScript.
- Provide a secure and user-friendly admin panel through Filament.
- Keep the database structure clear, consistent, and extendable.
- Prevent uploaded media from significantly degrading performance.
